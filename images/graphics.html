    Lions Den Gym

    Advertising materials for Lions Den Gym, a growing company with 2 locations founded by Khalid Ishmal.[more]

    Brief: Design advertising materials for the opening of the refurbished Gym, these have to appeal to the various new groups the gym is looking to attract with areas such as womens only sections and kids classes.

    Examples:

    Solution:LDG already has a set logo and style but it, up untill recently has been quite focused on men, I had to adjust this to being more appealing to other groups such as women and parents/kids while maintaining LDG's identity. This was dome by introducing some new colours and having a few different combinations depending on which group that piece of advertising was focused on. The bold shapes and lines and the use of some elements from the LDG branding allowed it to retain the overall LDG look throught.

    The Mermaid

    Redesign of the branding and stationery and continued advertising and marketing materials for The Mermaid London, a historical theatre and conferance venue in the heart of london.[more]

    Brief: To modernise the look of the company, bring it up to date while maintaining the feel of the building and the name and what it represents. To then design the following advertising materials and stationery required.

    Examples:

    Solution: Being called the Mirmaid and siruated on the River Thames, it seemed logical to link the logo to water, I also wanted to link it to its theatrical/musical nature. The idea of a kind of musical wave seemed to work well, it was simple and elegant and made an instantly recognisable and unique symbol that could be used in many ways throught their other material.
